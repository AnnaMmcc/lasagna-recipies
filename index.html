This is the best lasagna recipe you ever try! It is tastefyl and juicy. You are gonna love it.

Required ingredients:

  • Lasagna crusts
  • Mixed minced meat
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Spices
  • Tomato paste
  • Cheeses
  • Egg
  • Onion and garlic

How to make lasagna step-By-Step:

  1. Add oil to a deep pan and sautee onion and meat untill browned, 5 minutes then add garlic another minut.Then add potato paste and cook 3 minutes
  2. Add spices you want (salt,peper,paprika,sugar)cover and cook 5 minute.
  3. Fry the flour in a little oil and then add milk until it starts to boil.Mix and cook until the mass thickens.Add cheese and season.
  4. Grease the pen, coat with white sauce and arrange the lasagna.Cover with red sauce.Arrange the lasagna again.Arrange like this until the pen is ful.Sprinkle chees all over.
  5. Heat the oven to 250 degrees and bake for 30-45 minutes.
  6. Leave to cool for 10 minutes and serve.
  7. Bon apetit!
